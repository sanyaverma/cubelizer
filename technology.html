Our Technology

Our device gathers people positions with full range coverage in a shopping center. Using our in-house developed algorithms, the technology has the power to successfully track pathways of customers and monitor their behavior. Secured cloud based storing is used to capture the data in a private and safe manner. Moreover, as we do not store nor images, video neither personal data, there is no privacy issue and the solution is GDPR compliant.

Our system uses wireless connection and cloud infrastructure so wired network infrastructure and local servers are not required.
